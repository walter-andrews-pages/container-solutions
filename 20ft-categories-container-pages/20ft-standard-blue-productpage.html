20ft Standard Heights

Product Details


Our 20ft standard shipping container is a robust and reliable storage solution, designed to provide a secure and weather-tight environment for your goods. Constructed from 1.5mm thick corten steel, this container features:
- A sturdy frame and roof to withstand heavy loads and harsh weather conditions
- A secure locking system to protect your valuable cargo
- A durable and easy-to-clean floor to simplify loading and unloading
- A spacious interior with ample room for storing goods and equipment

Delivery Guide

General Specifications
- Length: 20ft (6.1 meters)
- Width: 8ft (2.4 meters)
- Height: 8.5ft (2.6 meters)
- Weight: approximately 2,500 kg (5,500 lbs)
- CSC (Convention for Safe Containers) certified
- ISO (International Organization for Standardization) certified

We offer nationwide delivery of our containers. The delivery cost is calculated based on the
distance from our depot to your location. To provide you with an accurate quote,
please provide us with your zip code. Our standard delivery options include:
- Ground-level delivery: Our driver will place the container on the ground at the delivery site.
- Liftgate delivery: Our driver will use a liftgate to lower the container to the ground.
Please note that additional fees may apply for deliveries to residential areas,
construction sites, or areas with limited access.
